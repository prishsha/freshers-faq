About FreshStart

FreshStart connects enthusiastic freshers with experienced professionals for mentorship and guidance. Founded in 2023, we empower emerging talent while offering seniors a platform to give back. Whether you're seeking career advice or eager to share your expertise, FreshStart fosters meaningful professional connections.
